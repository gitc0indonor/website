## 8. FAQ (faq.html)

### Hero
- **Headline:** FAQ
- **Subtitle:** Frequently Asked Questions
- **Breadcrumb:** Home > FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What is auto detailing?**
A: Auto detailing is the thorough cleaning, restoration, and finishing of a vehicle to produce a high-quality cleanliness and polish. Unlike a regular car wash, detailing involves meticulous attention to both the interior and exterior of your car, using specialized products and techniques to protect and enhance its appearance.

**Q: How long does a detailing service take?**
A: The duration depends on the package and vehicle condition:
- Exterior Wash & Wax: 1-2 hours
- Interior Detail: 2-3 hours
- Full Detail Package: 4-5 hours
- Ceramic Coating: 5-7 hours

**Q: Do I need to provide water or electricity for mobile detailing?**
A: No! Our mobile units are fully self-contained with our own water supply and power. All we need is access to your vehicle and a safe place to work.

**Q: How often should I get my car detailed?**
A: We recommend:
- Basic wash: Every 2 weeks
- Interior detail: Every 3-6 months
- Full detail: Every 6-12 months
- Ceramic coating maintenance: Every 12-18 months

### Booking & Payment

**Q: How do I book an appointment?**
A: You can book through our online booking system, call us at (555) 123-4567, or email us at info@weridecleaning.com. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and digital payment methods including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

**Q: Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?**
A: Yes! You can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled time at no charge. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a $25 fee.

**Q: Do you offer gift cards?**
A: Yes! Gift cards are available for all our services and make perfect gifts for car enthusiasts. You can purchase them through our website or by contacting us directly.

### Services & Products

**Q: What products do you use?**
A: We use only premium, professional-grade, eco-friendly products from trusted brands. Our products are safe for your vehicle's surfaces and the environment.

**Q: Is ceramic coating worth it?**
A: Absolutely! Ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection (12+ months), makes your car easier to clean, enhances gloss and shine, and protects against UV damage, oxidation, and minor scratches.

**Q: Can you remove scratches from my car?**
A: Yes! Our paint correction service can remove or significantly reduce most surface scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation. Deeper scratches may require additional treatment.

**Q: Do you offer any guarantees?**
A: Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll come back and make it right at no additional cost.

### Service Area

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We currently serve the greater metropolitan area including downtown and surrounding suburbs within a 30-mile radius.

**Q: Is there an extra charge for mobile service?**
A: No, mobile service is included in our pricing. There are no additional travel fees within our service area.
