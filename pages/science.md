# Science Behind Cognicit

Cognicit combines three well-researched ingredients:

- **Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA)** – a powerful antioxidant that supports mitochondrial function and protects neurons from oxidative stress. It also helps regenerate other antioxidants like vitamins C and E.
- **Citicoline (CDP-Choline)** – a precursor to acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter involved in memory and learning. Citicoline supports brain cell membrane integrity and neuroplasticity.
- **Beta-Cyclodextrin** – a cyclic oligosaccharide that improves the stability and solubility of active ingredients, protecting them from degradation and enhancing bioavailability.

These ingredients work synergistically to support cognitive functions such as memory, focus, and mental clarity. Each component has been studied in clinical and pre-clinical settings.

Our manufacturing process takes into account the technical sensitivities of these ingredients:
- ALA is heat-sensitive; we use low-speed encapsulation and light-controlled environments.
- Citicoline is hygroscopic; we prefer low-moisture HPMC capsules and controlled humidity.
- The ~800mg fill requires high-density granulation (roller compaction) to ensure consistent capsule fill.

Cognicia is produced in EU-GMP or equivalent facilities with full documentation: COA, stability data, and traceability.
