## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Style
- Clean, modern, medical-to-consumer aesthetic
- Color palette: forest green (#1a3a2a), gold (#c9a96e), white, cream
- Typography: Inter for body text, EB Garamond for headlines
- No stock footage clichés — use custom motion graphics or AI-generated visuals
- Subtle animations, not flashy transitions

### Voiceover
- Polish male voice, 30-40 years old
- Calm, confident, scientific-but-approachable tone
- NOT a "reklamowy" (advertising) voice — more like a knowledgeable friend
- Pace: moderate, with pauses at key stats

### Music
- Ambient electronic, subtle, builds slightly toward CTA
- No lyrics, no distracting melody
- Reference: Headspace/Calm app audio style

### Formats to deliver
- 16:9 (YouTube, product page) — 1:20
- 9:16 (Instagram Reels, TikTok) — 0:45 cut
- 1:1 (Facebook feed) — 0:60 cut
- Square caption version for LinkedIn

### AI Video tools to consider
- Runway Gen-3 (molecule animations, cross-section)
- Pika Labs (office scene, before/after)
- D-ID or HeyGen (avatar-based alternative)
- Canva Video (text overlays, transitions)

### Estimated cost
- DIY with AI tools: $0-50
- Freelance motion designer (Fiverr): $100-300
- Professional studio: $500-1500
